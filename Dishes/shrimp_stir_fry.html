Shrimp Stir Fry



Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen stir-fry vegetables
  • 20 uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined


Directions

step 1

Gather all ingredients.

step 2

Mix chicken stock, soy sauce, cornstarch, and garlic in a bowl; season with salt and pepper.

step 3

Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until oil shimmers; cook and stir

step 4

Add shrimp; cook and stir until shrimp begin to turn pink, about 3 minutes.

step 5

Stir chicken stock mixture into vegetable-shrimp mixture. Continue to cook and stir until
